Sweet Potatoes Burger

Description

These tasty burgers are whole lot healthier than your average burger but a whole lot tastier

These burgers may take a while to make but worth every minute of it once they are on the plate

Ingredients

  • Black Beans
  • Rolls
  • Sweet Potatoe
  • Lettuce
  • Onion
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Roast sweet potatoes for 1hr
  2. Mash up black beans, sweet potatoes and onion
  3. Form mash into burgers
  4. Fry burgers
  5. Toast bun construct lettcue, sauce and burger into dinner
