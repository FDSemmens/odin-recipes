Roast

Description

This is a tasty roast chicken with some lovely potatoes peas and pumpkin on the side

It is the perfect meal for a traditional Sunday Dinner

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Peas
  • Potatoes
  • Pumpkin
  • Oil
  • Seasoning to taste

Steps

  1. Rub chicken with oil
  2. Cut potatoes and pumpkin, put on tray drissle with oil
  3. Put chicken and veggies in oven for 1.5hrs
  4. Boil Peas
  5. Enjoy
